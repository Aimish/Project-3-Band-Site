The four members of Vivacious July have been friends since childhood. They've performed together for many years and music - of all kinds, and in all forms - is their passion. They love playing together in a wide range of settings, and they love introducing existing fans and new audience members to their music. More than anything, they work (and play) hard to encourage music appreciation in people of all ages.


Vivacious July "events" range the gamut from standard concerts (in smaller venues - not the Pepsi Arena!) to music festivals, open-mic nights, and performances in local venues of all types. Some of their favorite places to perform include the Troy Savings Bank Music Hall, the Troy Farmers Market, local harvest festivals, local schools, and Cafe Lena, in Saratoga Springs.


In addition to performances (as a quartet or other combinations, including solo presentations and performances), we also love to teach people of all ages about music, and to encourage in them a love for music. Because of this, many of our events are smaller, quiet events, in more "intimate" venues, where we can gather with smaller audiences and simply talk about our love of music and the music we perform at that event.
