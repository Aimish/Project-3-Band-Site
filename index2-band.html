Vivacious July's four musicians have deep roots in New York State's Capital District and have been close friends since childhood. They express a wide vocal range and depth, excelling on multiple instruments and imbuing their music with a delightful sense of mischief and humor. The band performs original pieces as well as beloved old standards, encouraging audience participation and engagement. Their music appeals to all ages and their performance events have been described as "...flat out FUN and ENGAGING."


The band believes, and works hard, to make their performances and events a "gathering of friends" and a celebration of Upstate New York's many special places and products. New fans/participants are warmly welcomed, and long-term followers enjoy the long-established sense of community reinforced by the band's lively, fun, multi-sensory events and performances.
